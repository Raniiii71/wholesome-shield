# WholesomeShield Terms and Conditions

Last updated: May 19, 2026

WholesomeShield is a Reddit Devvit moderation app for moderators who want to protect family-friendly communities from NSFW, adult promotional, spam, unsafe, or harmful content.

By installing or using WholesomeShield, you agree to use it only for lawful subreddit moderation and in accordance with Reddit's Developer Terms, Reddit's User Agreement, and Reddit's Moderator Code of Conduct.

## What the App Does

WholesomeShield may inspect submitted post titles, post bodies, comment text, links, usernames, flair text, Reddit NSFW status, and media URLs available through Reddit's Devvit APIs. When content matches configured safety signals, the app may remove the content, leave a moderator notice comment, send a private warning message, store a violation count, and ban a repeat violator from the subreddit.

## Moderator Responsibility

Moderators are responsible for deciding whether WholesomeShield is appropriate for their community. Automated moderation can make mistakes, so moderators should review removals and user appeals when needed.

WholesomeShield is provided as-is, without warranties of any kind. The app is designed to reduce unsafe or unwanted content, but it cannot guarantee that every violation will be detected or that every clean item will be allowed.

## Contact

For questions, issues, or removal appeals, contact the moderators of the subreddit where WholesomeShield is installed.
