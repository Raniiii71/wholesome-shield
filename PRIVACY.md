# WholesomeShield Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 19, 2026

WholesomeShield is a Reddit Devvit moderation app that helps moderators protect family-friendly communities from NSFW, adult promotional, spam, unsafe, or harmful content.

## Information Processed

WholesomeShield may process Reddit content and metadata available to the app through Reddit's Devvit APIs, including:

- post titles and bodies
- comment text
- submitted links and media URLs
- usernames and user IDs
- subreddit names
- post flair text
- Reddit NSFW status
- moderation actions taken by the app

## Information Stored

WholesomeShield stores limited moderation state in Reddit Devvit Redis, including violation counts, timestamps, last moderated content IDs, scheduled scanner job IDs, and recent scan fingerprints used to avoid duplicate warnings.

This data is used only to operate the moderation workflow, prevent duplicate actions, and apply repeat-violation rules.

## Information Sharing

WholesomeShield does not sell personal information. The app uses Reddit-hosted Devvit services and Reddit APIs to perform moderation actions. Information may be visible to subreddit moderators through normal Reddit moderation tools, logs, comments, private messages, or ban notes.

## Data Retention

Violation records are retained so repeat violations can be handled. Recent scan fingerprints expire automatically after seven days. Moderators may uninstall the app from their subreddit at any time.

## Third-Party Services

The current app does not send content to external AI or media-scanning providers. If external scanning is added later, this policy should be updated before that feature is released.

## Contact

For privacy questions, contact the moderators of the subreddit where WholesomeShield is installed.
